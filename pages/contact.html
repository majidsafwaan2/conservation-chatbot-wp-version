Getting to EcoVillage

Directions

EcoVillage of Loudoun County is located in Loudoun County, Virginia, approximately one hour from Washington, DC. The Marc train station is located four miles from our community, providing easy access to Washington, DC and surrounding areas.

Transportation

  • By Car: Take Route 7 or Route 50 to Loudoun County
  • By Train: Marc train station is 4 miles away
  • By Bus: Local bus service available
