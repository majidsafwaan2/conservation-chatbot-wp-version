Explore the layout and available lots at EcoVillage of Loudoun County

EcoVillage Layout

EcoVillage offers a variety of lot sizes and configurations to meet different needs and preferences. Our lots range from approximately one-quarter to two-thirds of an acre, providing plenty of space for homes, gardens, and outdoor living.

The community is designed with sustainability in mind, featuring walking trails, common areas, and natural spaces that connect all the lots together. Each lot is carefully positioned to maximize natural light, privacy, and access to community amenities.
