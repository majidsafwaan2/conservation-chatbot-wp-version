About The Farm

The JK Community Farm is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to providing fresh, organic produce to families in need throughout the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area. Founded in 2010, our farm has grown from a small community garden to a thriving agricultural operation that serves thousands of families each year.

Our mission is simple: grow healthy food and give it to people who need it. We work with local food pantries, shelters, and community organizations to ensure that fresh, nutritious produce reaches those who need it most.

We believe that access to healthy food is a basic human right, and we're committed to making that right a reality for everyone in our community.
