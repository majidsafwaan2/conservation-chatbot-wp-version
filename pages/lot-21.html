About Lot 21

Lot 21 is one of our most desirable lots, featuring a generous 0.5-acre size with a gentle slope that provides excellent drainage and beautiful views. The south-facing orientation maximizes natural light throughout the day, making it perfect for solar panels and passive solar design.

The lot is surrounded by mature trees and natural vegetation, providing privacy and a sense of seclusion while still being close to community amenities. The gentle slope allows for creative landscaping opportunities and can accommodate various home designs.

Lot Features

  • South-facing orientation for maximum sunlight
  • Gentle slope for excellent drainage
  • Mature trees and natural vegetation
  • Privacy and seclusion
  • Close to walking trails
  • Access to community amenities
  • Perfect for solar panel installation
  • Suitable for various home designs
